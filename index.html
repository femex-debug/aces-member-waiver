Participant Waiver & Release Agreement

Please read this agreement carefully before signing. By completing this form you are agreeing to legally binding terms including assumption of risk, release of liability, and indemnification of ACES Club Inc. and Russett Community Association.

1Assumption of Risk
I understand that participation in tennis activities — including lessons, training sessions, organized play, tournaments, and related events — involves inherent risks of injury. These risks may include slips, falls, collisions, equipment-related injuries, weather conditions, and other unforeseen hazards.

I voluntarily assume full responsibility for any risks of loss, property damage, or personal injury that may occur as a result of my participation.
2Release of Liability
In consideration of being permitted to participate in activities organized by Aces Club Inc., I release, waive, discharge, and covenant not to sue:
  • Aces Club Inc. and its officers, directors, board members, employees, volunteers, instructors, agents, and representatives
  • Russett Community Association (RCA) and its board members, officers, employees, management, agents, and representatives
…from any and all liability, claims, demands, actions, or causes of action arising out of or related to participation in tennis activities conducted at RCA facilities or any other location used by Aces Club Inc.
3Indemnification
I agree to indemnify and hold harmless Aces Club Inc. and Russett Community Association from any loss, liability, damage, or costs (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from my participation in activities or my violation of facility rules or policies.
4Compliance with Facility Rules
I agree to:
  • Follow all Russett Community Association rules and regulations regarding court use
  • Respect residents and shared amenities
  • Follow instructions from designated Aces Club organizers
  • Maintain sportsmanlike and respectful conduct at all times
Failure to comply may result in removal from participation.
5Medical Responsibility
I affirm that I am physically capable of participating in tennis activities. I understand that neither Aces Club Inc. nor Russett Community Association provides medical insurance coverage for participants. I am solely responsible for any medical expenses incurred as a result of participation.
6Media Consent
I grant Aces Club Inc. permission to use photographs or video taken during activities for nonprofit and promotional purposes.
7Acknowledgment
I have carefully read this agreement, fully understand its terms, and understand that I am giving up substantial legal rights by signing it. I sign this agreement freely and voluntarily.
